REVIEWS

Bester Potgieter

Review from a fighting game beginner:

I play lots of game from a wide variety of other genres but was never really into fighting games and I've been trying to get myself into it for a while. Tried SF6 and various other games on the PS catalog but nothing really interested me.

So I took a shot and just bought Tekken 8 without ever having played the series seriously before (played Tekken 5 with family back in the day but was super casual). Eventhough I only have 11 hours so far it definitely feels like the game I've wanted. It was easy enough for me to understand the basics but it's definitely going to take some time to get to a decent level.

I do think other newcomers like me wouldn't feel too overwhelmed and will definitely be having alot of fun once you go through understanding the basic stuff.
(opinion is subject to change in the future)

Nelson Bustamante Ordaz

Been a tekken fan since the first one.

Graphics have been bumped up immensely compared to 7. The visual effects for me are better than mk1 or street fighter 6.

The combat system has been changed significantly with the new heat meter. Able to extend combos or deal more damage when catching opponents with counters.

The storyline is very satisfying. Has an awkward build up to the final showdown but definitely surpasses heihachi vs kazuya.

The new characters Reina, Victor and azucena are very refreshing and exciting.

For me this should be fighting game of the year
