FAQS

Does Tekken 8 have crossplay?

Yes, there is full crossplay across PlayStation, Xbox, and PC.

Is Tekken 8 on PlayStation Plus?

Tekken 8 is not currently available on any of the PS+ tiers.

Is Tekken 8 on Xbox Game Pass?

Tekken 8 is not currently available on Xbox Game Pass for console or PC.

Tekken 8 platforms

Tekken 8 is available on PS5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC via Steam.
